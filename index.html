About this site

Who Shot Me? helps you find roadside motorcycle photographers around Derbyshire - whether they're live right now, coming up, or already wrapped up from a past ride.

Meet the creator

Hi, I'm Peter.

I'm a keen motorcyclist and GIS professional from the Peak District. I first got into riding on a red 2010 Honda CB125, and these days you'll usually find me out on an orange Honda CB500F with a pillion passenger… a Charmander soft toy. It's for the kids… honest!

After seeing the same question appear time and time again in motorcycle Facebook groups - "Who was the photographer at Cat & Fiddle?", "Does anyone know who was at Snake Pass today?" - I realised there had to be a better way.

That's what inspired me to create WhoShotMe.

The aim is simple: make it easy for riders to find photographers, and help photographers reach more riders. By bringing both together in one place, I hope to save riders from endless searching while giving photographers another way to showcase their work.

WhoShotMe is an independent project that I develop in my spare time, and it's continually evolving based on feedback from the motorcycle community. Every suggestion helps shape the platform and where it goes next.

Thanks for visiting, and I hope you find WhoShotMe useful.

Built by a rider, for riders and photographers.

Your privacy

Visiting this site: we keep a simple count of page visits and which photographer links get clicked, entirely within our own systems - no third-party analytics company is involved. No cookies, nothing stored in your browser, nothing that identifies you personally - just anonymous counts.

"My location" button: if you tap it, your browser will ask your permission directly - that's a conversation between you and your browser, not something WhoShotMe controls. If you allow it, your coordinates are used only to center the map on your device there and then; they're never sent to us, never stored, and never logged anywhere. Close the tab and it's gone.

Photographer listings: if you're listed, we store the details needed to run your listing - your name, contact email, logo and website links, and the shoot times/locations you submit. You manage this yourself via your own personal link; contact us (see below) to have your listing removed entirely. We keep this data for as long as you're listed, and delete it once you ask us to.

This runs on Google Sheets/Apps Script (data storage), GitHub Pages (hosting), the OS Data Hub, Esri, and OpenStreetMap (map tiles - whichever basemap you've got selected), and postcodes.io / OpenStreetMap's Nominatim (turning a typed postcode or place name into a map location) - each processes the minimum needed to make their part work.

Photographers: please only submit content you have the rights to post, and keep listings accurate - no illegal or offensive content. Listings that don't meet this may be removed.

Questions, or want your listing removed? Email us.
