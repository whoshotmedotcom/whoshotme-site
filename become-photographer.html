About this page

This page is how you sign up to list your shoots on Who Shot Me - the live map riders use to find out who was photographing them. Confirm your email and your personal dashboard is ready straight away, no approval needed from anyone.

Meet the creator

Hi, I'm Peter.

I'm a keen motorcyclist and GIS professional from the Peak District. I first got into riding on a red 2010 Honda CB125, and these days you'll usually find me out on an orange Honda CB500F with a pillion passenger… a Charmander soft toy. It's for the kids… honest!

After seeing the same question appear time and time again in motorcycle Facebook groups - "Who was the photographer at Cat & Fiddle?", "Does anyone know who was at Snake Pass today?" - I realised there had to be a better way.

That's what inspired me to create WhoShotMe.

The aim is simple: make it easy for riders to find photographers, and help photographers reach more riders. By bringing both together in one place, I hope to save riders from endless searching while giving photographers another way to showcase their work.

WhoShotMe is an independent project that I develop in my spare time, and it's continually evolving based on feedback from the motorcycle community. Every suggestion helps shape the platform and where it goes next.

Thanks for visiting, and I hope you find WhoShotMe useful.

Built by a rider, for riders and photographers.

A quick word on safety

WhoShotMe only exists to help riders find their own photos - it's not an invitation for anyone to push things because a camera's involved. Ride your own ride applies to everyone out there, riders most of all. If your presence at a spot ever seems to be encouraging risky riding (showing off, racing past, overtaking dangerously), please move on or shoot from somewhere less likely to have that effect. And obviously, shoot from a safe spot yourself, clear of traffic. Everyone gets home safe - that's the only rule that matters.

Your privacy

We store your name, contact email, and website/social link (if you gave one) so we can email you your dashboard link and let riders see who's listed. You manage all of this yourself afterwards via your own personal link.

We keep this data for as long as you're listed, and delete it once you ask us to.

This runs on Google Sheets/Apps Script (data storage) and GitHub Pages (hosting) - each processes the minimum needed to make their part work.

Please keep in mind

Only submit content you have the rights to post, and keep listings accurate - no illegal or offensive content. Please only sign up if you genuinely shoot roadside motorcycle photos yourself, not on someone else's behalf.

Questions, or want your data removed entirely? Email us.
